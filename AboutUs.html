Company history

lluminating Life insurance has a deep rooted history in the annals of Sri Lankan life insurance.  From humble beginnings to the Life Insurance giant we are today,  we have passed through milestones which are not just significant for us as a company,  but for the Sri Lankan life insurance industry as well,  setting the benchmark and leading by example.

Being the household name for life insurance for 34 years,  of which 17 have been the market leader,  it is evident that we have moved from strength to strength,  in rough seas as well as in calm waters and we will continue to sail forward.

Today

Undispute
With the dawn of a new decade,   lluminating Life insurance celebrated 17 years of Market Leadership and promises many more years of championing Life Insurance and aiming to protect all Sri Lankans.
Strength in Numbers
lluminating Life insurance became the fastest life insurance company to surpass the 100 Billion Life Fund mark,  and only the second life insurance company in Sri Lanka to achieve this feat,  ensuring financial protection for the assurance placed by our policyholders on us.

2010

A Separate Entity
Keeping up with the change in regulatory requirements the company became a separate entity as lluminating Life insurance Insurance Limited and was incorporated on the 22nd of April 2014.
Being The Pioneer
Clluminating Life insurance has always been at the forefront of innovation and in 2012 we launched Sri Lanka's first Online Life Insurance Plan.  We were also the first life insurer to introduce Tele-Underwriting to Sri Lanka and the first life insurance company to surpass the One Billion annual premium income.

2000

Moving Forward
In the year 2000 a separate brand icon was introduced for the life insurance division as Clluminating Life insurance and in 2004 we achieved the market leadership.
The now iconic loyalty programs,  the Pranama Scholarship scheme and Family Savari,  which are household names today,  were also introduced in 2001 and 2007 respectively.

1990

Achieving Success
In 1993 lluminating Life insurance produced the first MDRT qualifier in Sri Lanka and continued to further empower the sales force by introducing professional courses in 1995.  The company's foresight and vision enabled them to introduce the first pension product in 1999.

1930

Humble Beginnings
Ceylon Insurance Company was founded by Hugh Weerasekere and Cyril E.  S.  Perera.  It was the first Ceylonese Company to be registered under the Companies Ordinance of 1938.  The company commenced business on 3 April 1939,  providing life and general insurance services.
